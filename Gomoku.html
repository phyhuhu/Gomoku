Gomoku!

Instruction:

Gomoku, also called Five in a Row, is an abstract strategy board game. Two players alternate turns placing a piece of their color on an empty cell. The winner is the first player to form an unbroken chain of five pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game is a tie if the entire board fills up without a winner. The default board size is 10X10, which can be changed through "Set Up Board Size".
